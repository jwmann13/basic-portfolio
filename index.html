About Me

Hi! I'm Jeff! I have a Bachelors of Music in Music Theory and a Certificate of Music Business from the University of Georgia. I love music and keep a deep appreciation of the artform. I often try to expand my collection and understanding of music and my favorite tools to find music are Spotify, RateYourMusic, and YouTube. I also like record stores and have a vinyl collection.
